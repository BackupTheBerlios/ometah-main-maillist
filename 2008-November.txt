FIRST CALL FOR PAPERS

 

**********************************************************************

*

*                          MIC 2009

*    VIII Metaheuristic International Conference

*

*                      Hamburg, Germany

*                  July 13 - 16, 2009

*

*                 http://www.mic09.de

*

**********************************************************************

 

 

The 8th edition of the Metaheuristic International Conference 

(MIC 2009) will be held in Hamburg, Germany, from 13th to 16th

of July, 2009. 

 

As in the past, the conference will provide an opportunity

for researchers and practitioners in the field of metaheuristics

to present their latest results and findings in a fruitful 

and open-minded environment. 

 

Contributions on any topic in metaheuristics are welcomed.

 

 

IMPORTANT DATES

 

**********************************************************************

* Deadline of Submission of Extended Abstract       : March 15, 2009

* Deadline of Acceptance                            : April 30, 2009

* Deadline Submission Final Version                 : May 15, 2009

* Deadline Early Registration                       : May 31, 2009

* MIC 2009 in Hamburg                               : July 13-16, 2009

**********************************************************************

 

 

INVITED SPEAKERS

 

We plan to program three invited speakers. Invitations are currently 

under discussion.

 

REGISTRATION AND SUBMISSION

 

The registration and submission processes are already open to interested

people. We are accepting extended abstracts with a maximum length of

10 pages, written in English and typeset using the LaTeX template

provided at http://www.mic09.de/, under the "Submission" section (See

also  "Steps for Abstract Submission" in the same page.) 

 

Papers will be peer-reviewed and acceptance of the contributions 

will be based on originality of the work, suitability of the topic 

to the conference, and overall quality of the submission. 

 

POST-CONFERENCE VOLUME

 

In line with the preceeding editions of MIC, we plan to open 

invitations to submit high quality work for the post-conference 

volume on Metaheuristics. The volume will be published by Springer.

Further details about the post-conference submission will be provided soon.

                         CALL FOR PAPERS

 

                     Logistics Management 2009, 

                         2.-4. September, 

                      University of Hamburg

 

                        http://www.lm09.de

 

----------------------------------------------------------------------------
-------

 

The Logistics Management 2009 is an international conference 

addressing research experts and practitioners interested in logistics

management and related fields. The interdisciplinary 

exchange and the discussion of current trends, strategies and research 

outcomes are fundamental components of this conference.

 

Topics of interest include but are not limited to Supply Chain Management,
Collaborative Commerce, Reverse Logistics, IT-Systems in Logistics, 

Transportation-, Handling-, and Inventory Policies, Decentralized Logistics,


eBusiness as well as Education in Logistics.

 

Please submit your paper via the Conference homepage (www.lm09.de) not 

later than 31.01.2009. All submitted papers will go through a 

reviewing process and accepted papers will be published in the 

Conference proceedingsbook issued by Springer. A copy of the Conference 

Proceedings will await you at the beginning of the Conference.
