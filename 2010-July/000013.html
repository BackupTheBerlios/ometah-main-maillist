Dear list members,

As Stream Organizers for Metaheuristics at the next IFORS Meeting, that
will be held in Melbourne (Australia) on 10–15 July 2011, see
http://www.ifors2011.org/ , Celso Ribeiro and I are pleased to invite
you to organize and chair a session on a Metaheuristics theme of your
choice. One session comprises three to four invited papers. If you are
interested to do so, kindly let us know and you will get access to the
system.

Moreover, if you like to submit a paper for our stream, you can use the
abstract system on the website of the conference to do so.
